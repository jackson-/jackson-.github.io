Stack Overflow

Stack Overflow is a question and answer site for professional and enthusiast programmers. It's built and run by you (the programmers) as part of the Stack Exchange network of Q&A sites. I cannot tell you how many times this site has saved my ass. The wonderful thing about this resource is that it takes full advantage of the internet's community attributes. Chances are if you have a problem someone else in the world has that problem too. 

Hacker News

An invaluable community of tech experts delivery fresh content concerning the world of web & tech. If you're interested in many sides of technology and want to keep up with the most up to date topics this is your go-to spot. Ironic enough that one of the leaders in the field has a horrible website design but hey it works at the end of the day.

Neuro Lingustic Programming

Neuro Linguistic Programming (often shortened to NLP) is the study of how language effects can instill certain behavior in an individuals mind. I can't particularly point you anywhere but Richard Bandler's books and a good ol' Google search. Be careful on the Google search though as their is a lot of bonafide bull$#!% on the internet about it. I can't force you to believe it when you do your research but just try using it in your daily interactions with people while having an open mind about it. If you test these practices thinking they will fail I guarantee you they will.

The wonderful thing about Gamasutra is that it isn't just any video game site.
